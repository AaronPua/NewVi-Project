Growing the Finest Phalaenopsis Orchids

Why Lunfus

LUNFUS Inc. was established in Fall of 2011 by the acquisition of a 12-acre greenhouse property next to the town of Fonthill, Ontario. Premium potted phalaenopsis orchids incubated in Taiwan continue their spiking and blooming stages in Lunfus greenhouse facility under strictly controlled atmospheric conditions. The first batch of crop hit local markets in early Summer of 2012. With CGCP (Canadian Greenhouse Certification Program) in place, products were shipped as far as New Jersey, US, as well as local Greater Toronto Area. With over 40 varieties, Lunfus offers orchids in pot sizes of 7-cm pot (mini), 12-cm (regular) and 15-cm (jumbo!). Starting in 2013, the facility will consistently house over 70,000 orchids and produce approximately 10,000 pots monthly, year-round.
